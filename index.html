About Me

Born and raised in Georgia 🇬🇪, I’ve spent the last few years weaving together a life full of movement, meaning, and curiosity.

I hold degrees from Tbilisi State University and the University of Vienna (both with distinction 🎓✨).

Currently, I work as a metadata specialist in the public sector with over 6 years of experience, including roles in several ministries, 3 years in the NGO world, and study trips abroad — from the UN Headquarters in NYC 🇺🇳 to Microsoft’s cyberattack simulations 🛡️, and EU institutions in Brussels 🇧🇪.
